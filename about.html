About

My Story

My name is Ruel Erilla. When I founded The Better Batter Cakeology my mission was to use my artistic vision to bring my customers’ ideas to life with beautiful AND delicious food that everyone will love. Now, two years later, I have completed thousands of orders for my edible art. I am overjoyed by the reactions to my art from the community and look forward to continuing to hone my craft for years to come!
